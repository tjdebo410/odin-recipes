Roasted Salmon

Having a Roasted Salmon has never been Easier!

Salmon is a great, healthy and not overly expensive cut of fish

that makes for a quick weeknight meal!

Less than 30 minutes to make from start to finish!

Ingredient List

  • Olive Oil
  • Vegetables: Chopped Carrots, Broccoli, and Asparagus
  • Sauce: Soy Sauce, Lemon Zest, and Basil
  • Optional: Garnish with Parsley

Directions

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 350F. While that is going on chop your veggies.

    Make sure to wash your fish well and pat dry. Lightly brush olive oil on the fish and veggies.

    Once the oven is ready, put half an inch of water in the bottom of a 9x6 pan, cover with foil, and place fish and veggies.

    Make sure not to crowd the fish with veggies.

  2. In a small whisk together soy sauce, lemon zest, and veggies.

  3. Cook for 10-12 minutes depending on your oven.

    Once the fish has had a minute or two to cool you can brush your lemon sauce.

    At this point you can garnish with parsley if desired.
